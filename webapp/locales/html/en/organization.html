Reformer.network – A Safe Space for Real Change

Reformer.network is a closed space for people who want more than just discussion. We are a vibrant network for open exchange, collective action, and systemic change – beyond party politics, platform logic, and spirals of fear. Our goal: To strengthen an enlightened, dignified, and empowered civil society.

We see ourselves as a community of the courageous: People who want to think with clarity, speak with heart, and act together with others. For this, we create structures, safe spaces, and tools. At the center is the human being – with a real name, real identity, and real responsibility.

What Unites Us

  • Freedom and Truth
    We believe in the value of free speech, free choice, and free collaboration.
  • Community and Integrity
    We respect each other – regardless of background, profession, or worldview.
  • Impact over Noise
    Our platform is not a stage for self-promotion, but a workspace for ideas, projects, and real progress.

What Sets Us Apart

  • Access by invitation and verification only – for a trustworthy environment.
  • No algorithms, no tracking, no advertising – for true visibility.
  • Internal support for actions, research, and initiatives – for tangible impact.
  • Clear rules and respectful interaction – for long-term security.

Who We Are

Reformer.network is operated technically and organizationally by Redcap B.V. on behalf of Stichting Rudulin in Amsterdam. Behind it is a growing team of developers, moderators, visionaries, journalists, and activists from across the German-speaking world.

Why We Exist

Because many people feel: A new place is needed. Without censorship and division. With courage for truth and space for new solutions. Reformer.network is our contribution to that – decentralized, collective, and humane.
